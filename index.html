I am a Boya PostDoctoral Researcher at Peking University, working with Prof. Baoquan Chen. I received my Ph.D degree in Computer Science in 2015 from National University of Defense Technology, China, under supervision of Prof. Yueshan Xiong. From 2012 Sep. to 2014 Sep., I studied at Washington University in St.Louis as a visiting student supervised by Prof. Tao Ju.

My research interest mainly includes geometry modeling and processing, shape representation and generation, and image based reconstruction and localization.
